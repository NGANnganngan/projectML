About

Our sign language recognition model uses advanced machine learning algorithms to interpret and understand sign language gestures. With this model, we aim to bridge the communication gap between the hearing-impaired community and the rest of the world.

Features

  • Real-time sign language translation
  • High accuracy and reliability
  • Easy integration with existing applications

How It Works

Our model utilizes a deep learning architecture trained on a vast dataset of sign language gestures. It analyzes the movements and positions of the hands, fingers, and facial expressions to accurately recognize and interpret the intended message.
